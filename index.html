You Can’t Manage What You Don’t Measure

An organization uses key performance indicators (KPIs) as a measurement of efficiency and effectiveness of its operations.

You define measurement definition and target values of a particular KPI. You show actual values versus targets for business areas of interest.

KPI can give us insights into the areas that required our attention.

Dashboard is a visual display mechanism used to communicate performance (KPIs) at a glance. You make your own definition of your dashboard.


Performance dashboard is a tool that displays statistical data with a graph (chart) to cater strategic needs.

The dashboard is also one part of Decision Support Systems (DSS) that can interact with users, so users can obtain layered information needed to make decision.

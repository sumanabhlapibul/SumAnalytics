Data Visualization


Verbal communication is processed sequentially.
Visual Observation is consumed and processed in parallel.

Data transformation is a part of data visualization.
We transform data to get a better communication and understanding of our visuals.

Why Visualize data?

We visualize data to
  • help us understand the data
  • help us understand the phenomenon described by the data

Use of graphs

  • Bar Chart : Visualize how a quantity distributes across a set of categories.
  • Line Chart : Visualize how a quantity changes in relations to another quantity (typically time).
  • Scatter Plot : Visualize how a quantity relate to another quantity.
